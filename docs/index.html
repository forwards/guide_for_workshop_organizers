About this guide

We have put together this guide to help you organize and teach your first Forwards package workshop. We plan to cover the following:

  • How to coordinate logistics for your workshop
  • How to set up technical infrastructure
  • What to teach
  • Overview of curriculum

Thank you for your interest in organizing a Forwards workshop!
